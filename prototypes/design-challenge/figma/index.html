This is a known issue pattern with QBO Payroll Enhanced. Here's what's likely happening:

Root Cause Assessment:

Payroll runs submitted on Friday typically have a 2-business-day processing window. Since weekends don't count, clearing date would be Tuesday. A 3-day "pending" may indicate:

1. Bank processing delay — Employee's bank may have additional hold period.
2. Funding failure — Check payroll funding status in Payroll Tax Center.
3. ACH batch issue — Check Payroll > Payroll Tax > Tax Payments for batch status.

Recommended Steps:

1. Navigate to Payroll > Overview and check the run status
2. Look for error icons or warning banners
3. Verify funding account in Settings > Payroll Settings > Bank Account
4. If all correct, reassure customer — Tuesday processing is normal for Friday runs
