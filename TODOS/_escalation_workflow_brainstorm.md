## Agent Design System UI/UX
- Agent Selection: Choose from a diverse roster of AI agents, each with unique skills—like Creative Designer, Data Analyst, and Strategic Planner.
- Role Customization: Tailor each agent’s role to align with your project’s specific needs. Adjust parameters such as creativity level, analytical focus, and iteration speed.
- Workflow Settings: Define how your AI team collaborates, including communication protocols, task prioritization, and feedback loops.
- Project Integration: Seamlessly integrate your AI team into ongoing projects with just a few clicks, ensuring they complement your human team’s efforts.
- Diverse AI Roles: Specialized agents work together, each focusing on their strengths to optimize design outcomes.
- Real-Time Collaboration: Experience dynamic interaction as agents brainstorm, iterate, and refine ideas in real-time.
- Adaptive Learning: AI teams evolve with each project, learning from past experiences to enhance future performance.
